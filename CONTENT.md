1. **How do I reach Dimension 11:11 from Kasol bus stand?**
   We are located approximately 1.4 km from the Kasol bus stand in Old Kasol near Gungun Parking. You can walk (15-20 minutes) or take a local taxi. We can also arrange pickup if you let us know in advance.

2. **Is parking available at the property?**
   Yes, we offer free parking on-site - a significant advantage in Kasol where parking is often scarce and expensive.

3. **What is the check-in and check-out time?**
   Check-in time is 12:00 PM and check-out time is 11:00 AM. Early check-in or late check-out may be available upon request, subject to availability.

4. **Do you allow unmarried couples?**
   Yes, we welcome all guests including unmarried couples. Valid government ID proof is required at check-in for all guests.

5. **Is outside food allowed?**
   Outside food is not permitted at the property. However, our in-house cafe serves a variety of delicious meals throughout the day at reasonable prices.

6. **Do you have hot water?**
   Yes, all our rooms have 24/7 hot water supply - essential for the cold mountain climate.

7. **Is Wi-Fi available?**
   Yes, free Wi-Fi is available throughout the property. While speeds can vary due to the mountain location, it is suitable for regular browsing and work.

8. **What is the best time to visit Kasol?**
   Kasol is beautiful year-round. March to June offers pleasant weather perfect for trekking. July to September brings the monsoon with lush greenery. October to February is colder but magical, with possible snowfall.

9. **Can you help arrange treks and local activities?**
   Absolutely! Our caretaker can help arrange everything from trek guides for Kheerganga, Rasol, and other trails, to bike rentals, cab bookings, and restaurant reservations.

10. **Are pets allowed?**
    Unfortunately, we do not allow pets at the property to ensure a comfortable stay for all guests and maintain hygiene standards.
